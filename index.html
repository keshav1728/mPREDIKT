Introducing mPREDIKT

mPREDIKT, AI Based Predictive Maintenance

mPREDIKT is an intelligent software which can forecast a performance value ahead in the future by utilizing past behavior of the component. The software leverages state of the art techniques in the area of Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Signal Processing and Internet of Things.
mPREDIKT, a full-fledged tool, reduces unexpected machinery downtime and increases overall productivity, thus resulting more inflow. The system can be extended to incorporate sensory information so as to forecast more accurately by learning the correlation among different components.

How it helps?

01.

Reduces
Equipment Costs

Instead of replacement of the entire piece of equipment due to critical failure, a repair is made prior to failure and cost is minimized to the price of the component and the labor needed for the repair.

02.

Reduces
Labor Costs

When repairs are scheduled, the amount of time needed for repair is reduced because of a smaller number of component replacements instead of entire equipment replacement. .

03.

Reduces
Lost Production Time

Component only replacement is scheduled with production to take place during scheduled downtime. Unscheduled downtime may cost thousands of dollars per hour.

 

04.

Increases
Safety

Predictive maintenance would allow potential problems to be fixed before failure occurs, which would create safer driving conditions for employees and customers.

05.

Increases
Revenue

With less maintenance on good components and quicker repair of faulty components, repairs can be more effectively handled, thereby reducing repair time.

06.

Increases
Employee Time Efficiency

By identifying the precise repair task needed to correct deficiencies, as well as the parts, tools, and support needed to correct the problem can dramatically increase effective “wrench time.”

Our methodology at a
glance

Data Collection

Collect historical performance data and key data like Vibration, Pressure, Torque, Current using sensors or condition monitoring equipments.

Workflow Investigation

Other types of data like equipment's historical performance, Machine specifications and other operational conditions are evaluated.

Machine Learning Model

With advanced Machine Learning algorithms, mPREDIKT provides high and effective accuracy, and can predict failures two months in advance.

Insights and Warnings

A real-time visualization platform is provided to customize the settings and predict the health of all the equipments individually to prevent future failures.
